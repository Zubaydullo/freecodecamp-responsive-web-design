Stay hungry, Stay foolish

Here's a time line of Steve Jobs life:

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, California.
  • 1976 - Founded Apple Computer Inc. with Steve Wozniak.
  • 1985 - Founded NeXT Inc.
  • 1986 - Acquired The Graphics Group (later renamed Pixar).
  • 2000 - Became CEO of Apple Inc.
  • 2010 - Apple launched the iPad.
  • 2011 - Steve Jobs passed away.

Steve Jobs was a visionary, a pioneer in the world of technology. He was an entrepreneur, inventor, and industrial designer. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc. He was also the co-founder and CEO of Pixar Animation Studios. He was a charismatic leader who inspired people to think differently and to change the world. He was a true genius who revolutionized the way we live, work, and communicate. He will always be remembered as one of the greatest innovators of our time.
